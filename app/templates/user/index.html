Luzon

Visayas

Located in the central part of the archipelago, is famous for its stunning beaches, vibrant festivals, and rich culture heritage. This region includes world-renowned destinations like Boracay and Cebu, offering a mix of natural beauty and historical significance.

Mindanao

The southernmost island group, boasts vast natural resources and a wealth of cultural diversity. It is known for its lush forests, scenic landscapes and its vibrant indigenous communities. Mindanao is a region of rich cultural interactions, where different ethnic groups and traditions converge, making it a unique part of the Philippine identity.
